FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the most common questions our customers ask.

How do I port my number to Stackly?

Simply SMS "PORT <your 10-digit mobile number>" to 1900 from your current number. You'll receive a Unique Porting Code (UPC). Visit any Stackly store or apply online using this code. The process takes 3–7 working days.

What documents do I need for a new SIM?

You need one valid government ID (Aadhaar, Passport, Voter ID, or Driving Licence) and one address proof document. Aadhaar alone is sufficient for both identity and address. The entire process can be done online via the Stackly app with video KYC.

How do I upgrade from 4G to 5G?

To use 5G, you need a 5G-compatible device and a Stackly Pro or Ultra plan. If you're on Basic, simply upgrade your plan via the app. If your SIM is older than 2 years, you may need a free SIM replacement, which can be requested in the app.

Can I use my plan while roaming internationally?

International roaming must be activated. Ultra plan users get 25-country roaming included. For all other plans, purchase a roaming pack via the Stackly app before travelling. Packs start at ₹599 for 5 days in popular destinations.

How do I pay my bill online?

You can pay via the Stackly app (UPI, Cards, Net Banking, Wallets), our website, or at any Stackly store. We also support auto-pay — set it up once and your plan renews automatically without any action from your side.

What is Stackly's refund policy?

For any billing error or duplicate charge, we offer a full refund within 7 business days. Plan recharges are non-refundable once activated. For disputes, contact our support team with your transaction ID and we will investigate immediately.
