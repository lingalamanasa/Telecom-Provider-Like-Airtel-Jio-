Our Story

Born to Bridge India's Digital Divide

Founded in 2015, Stackly Telecom started with a bold vision: provide every Indian with access to affordable, fast, and reliable internet. What began as a small startup in a Mumbai garage has grown into a telecom giant with 50 million subscribers.

We were India's first operator to launch 5G Standalone (SA) technology in 2022, and since then, we have continuously pushed the boundaries of what's possible in mobile connectivity.

Today, Stackly serves consumers, small businesses, and Fortune 500 enterprises — all with the same commitment to quality, transparency, and innovation.
